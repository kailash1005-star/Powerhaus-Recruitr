# Recruitr — One-Pager for Staffing Sales Discussion
### Use during live demo · talk-track format · vendor- & platform-neutral

---

## The Pitch in One Line
**Recruitr is a team of AI agents that runs your hiring lifecycle end-to-end — on the ATS/CRM you already own. You don't switch systems; your recruiters just stop losing the day to admin.**

---

## The Problem We Solve (open here)
- Recruiters spend **~80% of the day on admin**, not placing people — ~**17.7 hrs of overhead per vacancy**.
- Keyword/Boolean filters **miss good fits** — "Product Lead" never shows up for a "Product Manager" search.
- **~75% of the candidates already in your database are never looked at again** — money sitting idle.
- Disconnected tools leak **15–25% of margin** before an invoice is sent.

## What Recruitr Does (the agentic lifecycle — walk the funnel)
- **JD Parser** — reads the role, extracts skills, experience, location *(Live)*
- **Candidate Search** — scores & sources every active candidate *(Live)*
- **Rediscover** — re-mines your *existing* database for past-best matches *(shipping)*
- **Match & Rank** — ranks candidates with a **reason for every decision** *(shipping)*
- **Personalize → Outreach** — drafts and sends tailored messages, tracks replies *(shipping)*
- **Schedule → Submit** — books interviews, builds the client-ready submittal *(roadmap)*

## Why Recruitr Wins (the 3 differentiators)
- **Neutral, not rip-and-replace** — plugs into your current stack; keep your data, contracts, workflow.
- **Semantic, not keyword** — matches on *meaning*, surfacing fits your ATS buries today.
- **Explainable & auditable** — every ranking has a plain-language "why," and a human always decides.

## The Outcomes That Matter (the ROI)
- **Recruiters back to selling** — flip the 80% admin into placement time.
- **Faster time-to-fill** — win the placement before competitors source it.
- **Recover leaking margin** — re-mining the database hires **~4× faster at 40–60% lower cost-per-hire**.
- **More wins from what you already have** — past finalists convert **~3× the rate** of fresh applicants.

## What You'll See in the Demo (the live moment)
- Drop in a job description → watch agents parse, source, and **rediscover** from the database.
- See a **ranked shortlist with evidence** — not a keyword score, a real reason per candidate.
- One CV → **client-ready submittal in ~60 seconds** (vs. 20–40 min by hand).

## Built for Trust (raise before they do)
- **GDPR / data stays yours**, fully logged — no training on your data.
- **EU AI Act–ready** (hiring = high-risk) and **bias-controlled** (protected attributes removed before scoring).
- **Human-in-the-loop by design** — defensible to your works council, DPO, and the regulator.

## The Ask (close here)
- **30-day pilot on one desk. No system switch. We measure the lift together.**
- If the numbers aren't there, you've lost nothing. If they are, you've found your edge in the tightest labour market in a generation.
