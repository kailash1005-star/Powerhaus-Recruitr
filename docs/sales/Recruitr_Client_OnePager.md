## The reality on your desk today

You already know the squeeze. Demand is enormous — in Germany alone roughly **628,000 roles sit
unfilled and the average vacancy stays open ~299 days** (*German Federal Employment Agency, 2025*),
with a shortfall of up to **7 million workers by 2035** (*German Economic Institute, IW*). The work
is there. Winning it and filling it fast is the hard part.

And three things quietly cost you placements and margin every week:

- **Good candidates get missed.** **88% of executives admit qualified people are screened out**
  simply because their CV doesn't match a job's exact wording (*Harvard Business School & Accenture,
  Hidden Workers, 2021*). Keyword search buries them.
- **Your systems leak money.** Disconnected, manual tools cause an estimated **15–25% margin
  leakage**, and **5%+ of billable revenue** is lost to preventable errors (*Staffing Industry Analysts*).
- **Getting it wrong is expensive.** A single bad hire costs **at least 30% of first-year earnings**
  (*U.S. Department of Labor*); replacing someone runs **50–200% of salary** (*SHRM*).

The talent and the clients are within reach. Manual, keyword-driven, disconnected tools lose them.

## What Recruitr does for you

Recruitr is a growth engine that works **both sides of your desk** — and runs on the data and
contacts you already have. No new system to live in.

**It brings you clients.**
Recruitr continuously scans job boards for companies hiring in **your** target industries,
qualifies them automatically (right industry, right size, screens out other agencies), and hands you
the **HR and hiring decision-makers to pitch** — with personalized outreach drafted for you.

**It fills the roles — from your own bench first.**
Drop in your CV database and paste a role. Recruitr returns a **ranked shortlist of best-fit
candidates by meaning, not keywords** — surfacing the strong people your current search misses — each
with a **match score, the reasons they fit, and any gaps** you can defend to your client.

**It sources new talent when you need it.**
No match in your pool? Recruitr searches the **public market (LinkedIn / Apollo)** for matching
profiles and ranks them for you.

**It reaches out for you.**
Warm, professional, personalized emails to candidates and clients — drafted and ready to send.
